TrialSense

Complete IOT platform
TrialSense is an IOT platform for capturing wearable device data in clinical trials. Our service can be deployed to all trials having endpoints for frequent remote vital sign measurements. Also as a complement for ePRO data: Using a well documented wearable for vital sign measurements opens new kind of possibilities to affect biases (recall bias, state bias etc.) as well as getting option to have ePro data classified with QC flagging.
Why we are different?
Measuring a physical quantity is an indirect measurement thru some other (usually electrical) quantity and involves a model error in relation between two quantities. While adding other error sources such as RMSE (Root Mean Square Errors), measurement errors (including variations of wearable deployments, non ideal characteristics of sensors) obviously lot of effort is required to have meaningful outcomes using wearables for endpoints in clinical trial. TrialSense is the first end to end IOT platform for wearables designed to address mentioned obstacles in clinical trials.
How does it work?
Our unique patch wearable measures ECG, Respiration and Core Temperature. MQTT telemetry and our scalable IOT frontends allows deployments at massive scale. Raw data can be stored into our HIPAA compliant cloud datalake or optionally into external datawarehouse pointed by CRO or investigator site. Our realtime analysis features provides alarms for desired events as well as perioidic reports based on study protocol. Optionally our realtime analysis storages can be configured to store data only for short perioids while raw data is persisted permanently into our datalakes or customer pointed datawarehouse resulting clear ownership of the data.
